One Of Us Is Lying

One of Us Is Lying is a popular young adult mystery written by Karen M. McManus. She is known for writing suspenseful teen novels that mix high school life with crime and secrets. McManus became widely recognized after this book was published in 2017, and it quickly became a bestseller. Many readers enjoy her stories because they include surprising twists, interesting characters, and mysteries that keep people guessing until the end.

The book One of Us Is Lying is about five high school students who go to detention together, but only four of them leave alive. The student who dies, Simon, ran a gossip app that exposed other students’ secrets. After his death, the four remaining students become suspects in the investigation. Each of them has something to hide, so the police and their classmates start to wonder who is telling the truth. As the story continues, secrets are revealed and the mystery of what really happened in detention slowly unfolds.
